About

About Us

Sharda Shaikshanik & Samajik Sanstha established in 2009 with a devoted team of trustees. We are glad that Sharda Santha has become a well recognized institution for imparting quality education owing to the constant, sincere and dedicated efforts of staff who have been channelizing all the efforts towards strengthening of these outcomes are evident. We believe in quality education and every learner is equal. We not only add skills to the learners but also impart moral values to them. Our institution, within a very short span has established a prestigious name in the field of education.

Sharda Shaikshanik & Samajik Sanstha well known as Vidyadhan was established by Prin.Dr. Kishor Bhika Patil with a vision to provide values added educational platform to society in multiple disciplinary right from Nursery to Post Graduation programs enabling all our students to achieve freedom through Education. Mrs. Sharda K. Patil a woman with golden dreams & enthusiasm of converting dreams into reality, is the President of Vidyadhan Educational Institute. She is associated with a large number of Educational, Social activities in Maharashtra. Vidyadhan has created academic ambience vibrant and enjoyable. It keeps its vision and mission in focus.  It keeps its vision and mission in focus. It instills in students a sense of commitment and devotion to serve humanity. It inculcates values that prepare students for academic excellence through various ways of analytical thinking, work ethics, devotion and dedication. We nurture qualities like leadership, creativity, aesthetic sense, discipline, compassion, life skills and soft skills. We always try to groom personality of our students by organizing various co-curricular, extracurricular and sports activities.

Vidyadhan educational institute is situated at a very approachable location near Dhule District Stadium, Valwadi, Dhule 424005. Sharda Shaikshanik & Samajik Sanstha now growing with four streams as Vidyadhan English Medium School, Vidyadhan Science & Commerce College affiliated to K. B. C. North Maharashtra University, Jalgoan for regular academic courses, Vidyadhan Arts, Science & Commerce College affiliated to Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nashik for distance education and Vidyadhan Sarvajanik Vachanalay granted by Government of Maharashtra.

Events

Blood Donation Camp

Sharda S & S Sanstha organizes Blood Donation camp by NSS Volunteers.

Cross-Country Race

Cross-Country race organized under Kavayatri Bahinabai Chaudhari North Maharashtra University, Jalgaon as inter-college sport competition.

Inter-College Sports

Kavayatri Bahinabai Chaudhari North Maharashtra University, Jalgaon organizes annual sport events for all affiliated colleges. In Year 2020-2022, these matches conducted by Vidyadhan College, Dhule.

Rashtriya Ekta Divas

On the occasion of Birth Anniversary of Sardar Vallabbhai Patel Vidyadhan college faculties celebrated Rashtriya Ekta Divas.

Constitution Day

NSS & Student association pay tribute to all Martyr and read Constitution of India in Sharda S & S Sanstha's campus.

Vaccination Camp

In this Pandemic due to COVID-19, Sharda S & S Sanstha organized Vaccination camp for all the faculties, students of the Institution.
